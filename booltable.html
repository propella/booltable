booltable : A simple boolean table generator

 
0: false, 1: true, -: not, +: or, *: and, =>: imply
A, B, C, ... : variables (only one character)

-- examples --
A+B = A or B
A*B = A and B
P=>Q = P implies Q
-P+Q = Definition of impliy
-(P*(-P+Q))+Q = Modus ponens
(A*-B)+(-A*B) = XOR
(P=>Q)*(Q=>R)=>(P=>R)
